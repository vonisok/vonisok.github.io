This site is now optimized for **solo founders and indie hackers building AI-first products**.

## Pick your track

### 1) Validate a problem fast
- Talk to 10 target users before writing code.
- Capture repeated pain points and quantify urgency.
- Ship a landing page with a clear promise and waitlist CTA.

### 2) Build a tiny MVP
- Keep scope to one painful job-to-be-done.
- Use your **Idea Map** to store assumptions, screenshots, and notes.
- Ship in 7–14 days, not 7–14 weeks.

### 3) Get first paying users
- Offer done-with-you onboarding for your first 5 customers.
- Turn support conversations into product requirements.
- Prioritize retention signals over vanity metrics.

### 4) Systemize growth
- Document acquisition channels that can be repeated weekly.
- Track activation, retention, and conversion in one place.
- Create a feedback loop from users → roadmap → releases.


### 5) Build your knowledge base
- Use the **Books** tab to organize reading lists by category.
- Attach first-page preview images and PDF files for each title.
- Keep notes in Idea Map and connect books to active projects.
