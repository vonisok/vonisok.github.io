Instead of a traditional archive, this section is a **review system** for builders.

## Monthly review cadence

### Week 1 — Direction
- What problem are we solving this month?
- Which user segment is highest priority?
- What assumptions must be validated first?

### Week 2 — Shipping
- What got shipped?
- What got delayed and why?
- What can be removed to reduce complexity?

### Week 3 — Feedback
- What did users struggle with?
- Which requests are repeating?
- What did users *actually* value?

### Week 4 — Decisions
- What will we double down on next month?
- What are we pausing or deleting?
- What is one measurable target for the next cycle?

## Founder rule
If something has not helped users in 30 days, either simplify it or cut it.
