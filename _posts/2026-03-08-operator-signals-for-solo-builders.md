## Signal tracking beats random scrolling

A founder who tracks the right signals weekly can out-execute a bigger team with more noise.

## High-value weekly signals

### User behavior signals

- Time-to-first-value changes
- Retention drop-offs by segment
- Repeated support friction points

### Product economics signals

- Cost per successful workflow
- Error rate in core flows
- Net margin by highest-usage feature

### Market movement signals

- Competitor pricing shifts
- New distribution channels in your niche
- Customer language changes in interviews

## Suggested weekly cadence

1. Monday: define two signals to monitor.
2. Wednesday: review early movement.
3. Friday: decide one concrete action.

## Rule

Do not track signals that do not trigger a decision.
