## Context date

This snapshot reflects the landscape as of **March 8, 2026**.

## What matters now

### 1. Reliability is becoming the moat

Many products can generate text. Fewer can deliver reliable outcomes.
The gap is moving from model novelty to operational quality.

### 2. Buyers want workflow impact, not model buzz

Teams care about cycle time reduction, error reduction, and better throughput.
Pitch outcomes, not architecture.

### 3. Cost discipline is now a product feature

Users increasingly notice output quality relative to price.
Teams with transparent value-to-cost ratios will win trust faster.

## What to do this month

1. Audit your most expensive workflow.
2. Reduce one unnecessary model/API call path.
3. Improve one reliability bottleneck users already complain about.
4. Publish one proof point tied to a measurable user outcome.
